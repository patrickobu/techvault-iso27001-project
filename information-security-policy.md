# Information Security Policy

**Organisation:** TechVault Ltd
**Document:** Top-Level Information Security Policy
**Version:** 1.0
**Approved By:** CEO
**Date:** 2025

## Policy Statement

TechVault Ltd is committed to protecting the confidentiality,
integrity, and availability of all information assets entrusted
to us by our clients. Information security is a business priority
and the responsibility of every employee.

## Objectives

1. Protect client documents from unauthorised access, disclosure,
   alteration, or destruction
2. Meet all contractual security obligations to clients
3. Comply with all applicable laws and regulations including GDPR
4. Maintain ISO 27001:2022 certification
5. Continuously improve our information security management system

## Responsibilities

**All employees must:**
- Complete annual information security awareness training
- Report suspected security incidents immediately
- Follow all information security policies and procedures
- Never share credentials or access information with others
- Lock workstations when leaving them unattended

**Managers must:**
- Ensure their team understands and follows security policies
- Report security incidents involving their team
- Participate in access reviews for their team's accounts

**The CISO must:**
- Maintain and improve the ISMS
- Report to the board on information security performance quarterly
- Ensure all security risks are identified and managed

## Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually and following any significant
change to TechVault's operations or threat landscape.
