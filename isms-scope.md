# ISMS Scope

**Organisation:** TechVault Ltd
**Standard:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2025

## ISMS Scope Statement

The Information Security Management System (ISMS) of TechVault Ltd
covers all information assets, processes, and personnel involved in
the development, operation, and support of the DocuVault cloud
document management platform.

## In Scope

- DocuVault platform development and operations
- AWS cloud infrastructure hosting DocuVault
- All 80 employees and contractors with access to client data
- London office and remote working environments
- All client data stored on the DocuVault platform
- Third-party suppliers with access to DocuVault systems

## Out of Scope

- TechVault's internal HR system (no client data processed)
- Marketing and sales activities (no client data access)

## Justification

The scope is limited to the DocuVault platform because this is
the system that processes client confidential data and is the
primary driver for ISO 27001 certification.

## ISMS Objectives

- Protect the confidentiality of client documents
- Ensure the integrity of all data stored on DocuVault
- Maintain DocuVault availability per committed SLAs
- Comply with all applicable legal and contractual requirements
- Continuously improve information security performance
